About

A design-build firm that owns the outcome

North American Modular engineers, oversees, and delivers custom modular structures for defense, data centers, and critical infrastructure. We hold the contract, control the build, and stand behind every unit we ship.

Our Model

Engineering and oversight, in-house

NAM is a design-build operation, not a broker. Our ops and design team owns the engineering, the specifications, and the oversight of every phase of construction — from first drawing to final inspection.

We build through a vetted network of fabrication partners, which keeps us lean and fast without the fixed cost of idle plant. But make no mistake about where responsibility sits: we engineer the unit, we control the build, we inspect the work, and we carry the warranty. The customer holds one contract — with us.

It's the same logic as a general contractor who doesn't pour his own concrete but is still the single party accountable for the building. The fabrication network is our capacity. The accountability is ours alone.

Built for the buyer who can't afford a weak link

The organizations we build for — defense programs, data center operators, infrastructure and energy contractors — answer to deployments, uptime, and inspectors. A vendor that sources a unit and disappears is a liability to them, not a partner.

So we built NAM to be the opposite of that. One engineering team. One point of accountability. One warranty, carried by us. We don't build homes, and we don't run a catalog — every structure is engineered to a specification and overseen by the people whose name is on the contract.

That's the whole proposition: the reliability of a single accountable builder, delivered through a model lean enough to move at the speed your project actually needs.
