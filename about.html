Simplifying container sourcing for commercial projects

North American Modular is a container distribution company based in Austin, Texas. We connect businesses with factory-direct container sourcing from our network of suppliers and certified fabricators.

Our Approach

One partner for sourcing

Container procurement is fragmented. Businesses typically navigate multiple vendors—each with their own pricing structures, lead times, and quality standards.

We consolidate that process. Our team manages vendor relationships, coordinates with fabricators, and handles quality verification. You get a single point of contact and straightforward pricing.

Whether you need standard shipping containers or pre-modified commercial units, we source them from our network so you can focus on your project.

Our Network

Factory-direct relationships

We maintain direct relationships with container suppliers and certified fabrication facilities, allowing us to source units at competitive pricing with shorter lead times than traditional distribution channels.

Our supplier network spans domestic and international sources, giving you access to a wide range of container types and modification options.

Our relationship with Bob's Containers

North American Modular operates as the commercial distribution arm of NAM Container Systems, the parent company of Bob's Containers.

While Bob's Containers serves residential customers and smaller-scale projects, North American Modular focuses on commercial clients, larger orders, and bulk sourcing.

This structure allows us to leverage shared vendor relationships while providing the dedicated attention that commercial distribution requires.
